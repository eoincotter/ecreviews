About the Website

My motivation for the theme of this website was my view that smart devices, particularly smartphones, are taking over our lives in a negative way. I also believe that smart devices, given the ubiquity and democratisation of information that they provide, have great potential to empower oneself through self-growth and development, however this potential is not being reached. This website has two main sections - Blog and Reviews. The Blog discusses ways we can turn our smartphone usage into a positive aspect of our lives, while also identifying some negative effects of the digital age, and ways we can overcome these. The Reviews section provides book reviews, which I am keen to continue as I believe reading physical books is a great way to escape from the digital world where the potential and temptation of connectivity is constant, yet rarely needed.
